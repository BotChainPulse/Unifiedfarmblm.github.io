💡 Broiler Quick Tips

  • Start with 50-100 chicks if you're new to broiler farming
  • Feed represents 65-70% of your total production cost
  • Target mortality under 5% for healthy profits
  • Pre-sell to hotels/restaurants before Week 5

🥚 Layer Farm Input Details

💡 Layer Quick Tips

  • Layers start laying at 18-20 weeks
  • Peak production is 85-90% in prime months
  • Calcium supplements improve shell quality
  • Clean water is essential for egg production

🐓 Local Chicken Farm Input Details

💡 Local Chicken Quick Tips

  • Local chickens are hardy but grow slower
  • Free-range reduces feed costs significantly
  • Market price is higher than broilers per kg
  • Good for organic/health-conscious buyers
