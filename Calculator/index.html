💡 Local Chicken Quick Tips

  • Local chickens are disease-resistant and need fewer vaccines
  • Allow free-range during day, shelter at night for protection
  • Supplement with kitchen scraps and grains to reduce feed costs
  • Local chicken meat and eggs sell at premium prices in Uganda
  • Keep a ratio of 1 rooster to 10 hens for natural breeding
