Our Story

From a small backyard flock to one of Uganda's most trusted family poultry farms — this is the UnifiedfarmBLM journey.

How It All Started

UnifiedfarmBLM was born from a simple dream: to provide Ugandan families and businesses with fresh, high-quality poultry products while building a sustainable farming legacy for our children.

Founded by Reagan Lutwama Nsimbe, UnifiedfarmBLM started as a small backyard operation with just a handful of layer hens. Today, it has grown into a full-scale poultry farm serving customers across Kampala, Entebbe, Jinja, and beyond.

The name BLM carries deep meaning — it stands for Basuuta, Lutwama, and Muyingo, the names of Reagan's children. Every egg, every chick, and every chicken that leaves our farm carries the promise of a father building something meaningful for his family and community.

We believe in quality over quantity, community over competition, and sustainability over shortcuts. Our farm is not just a business — it's a testament to what hard work, family values, and respect for the land can achieve.

👨‍🌾

Reagan Lutwama Nsimbe

Founder & CEO, UnifiedfarmBLM

Reagan is a passionate entrepreneur and poultry farmer based in Uganda. With years of hands-on experience in poultry management, he has built UnifiedfarmBLM from the ground up into a trusted brand for farm-fresh eggs, local chicken, and day-old chicks.

When he's not on the farm, Reagan is exploring digital entrepreneurship — building the BotChain Pulse ecosystem, developing mobile apps, and sharing farming knowledge through his blog to help other Ugandan farmers succeed.

Vision: To make UnifiedfarmBLM the most trusted poultry brand in East Africa while empowering 1,000+ farmers through knowledge sharing and quality supplies.
