About Unified Farm BLM

Welcome to Unified Farm BLM, a family-run sustainable agriculture project located in Mpigi, Uganda. We are passionate about producing premium organic poultry products and high-quality Ugandan coffee while empowering our local farming community.

Our Story

Unified Farm BLM was founded with a simple mission: to provide healthy, farm-fresh poultry products to families across Uganda while promoting sustainable farming practices. What started as a small backyard operation has grown into a trusted source for fresh organic eggs, day-old chicks, brooded chicks, and mature layers.

We believe that every family deserves access to fresh, nutritious food. That is why we raise our chickens with care, feed them quality nutrition, and ensure they receive proper vaccinations and health checks.

What We Do

Fresh Organic Eggs

Farm-fresh brown eggs collected daily from free-range hens. Rich in nutrients and flavor, priced at UGX 15,000 per tray.

Day-Old Chicks

Healthy, vaccinated broiler and layer chicks ready for your farm. Available at UGX 3,500 each.

Brooded Chicks (1 Month)

Mature Layers

Point-of-lay hens that are vaccinated and prepared for immediate egg production.

Organic Chicken Manure

Natural fertilizer rich in nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium for your crops.

Farm Consultation

Expert advice on poultry housing, feeding, disease prevention, and business planning.
