Privacy Policy

Learn how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Effective Date: January 2025

Unifiedfarm BLM ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website unifiedfarmblm.com and is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or place an order with us.

Information We Collect

When you interact with our website or place an order, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Name -- to identify you and personalize our communication
  • Email address -- to send order confirmations, updates, and respond to inquiries
  • Phone number -- to coordinate orders, deliveries, and provide customer support
  • Delivery address -- to fulfill your orders accurately

We only collect information that you voluntarily provide to us.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders for eggs, chicks, and other farm products
  • To communicate with you about your orders, deliveries, and inquiries
  • To provide customer support and respond to your questions
  • To send occasional updates about our products, farm news, or promotions (only with your consent)
  • To improve our website and services

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies -- small text files stored on your device -- to improve your browsing experience. We may also use basic analytics tools to understand how visitors use our website, which pages are most visited, and how we can improve our online presence.

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. However, this may affect some features of the website.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to operate our business efficiently:

  • WhatsApp -- We use WhatsApp for order communication and customer support. When you contact us via WhatsApp, your phone number and messages are processed according to WhatsApp's Privacy Policy.
  • Payment processors -- If online payments are accepted, they are processed through secure third-party payment providers.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Protection

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is stored securely and access is limited to authorized personnel only.

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
