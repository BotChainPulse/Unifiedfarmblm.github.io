Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver?

Yes! We offer local delivery within Mpigi district and can arrange transport to Kampala for larger orders. Delivery fees depend on distance and order size.

What is your minimum order?

For eggs, minimum order is 1 tray (30 eggs). For chicks, minimum is 10 birds. We offer discounts for bulk orders of 50+ chicks or 10+ trays of eggs.

Are your chicks vaccinated?

Yes! All our day-old chicks receive Marek's disease vaccination at hatch. We also provide vaccination schedules for follow-up doses (Newcastle, Gumboro, etc.).

Can I visit the farm?

Absolutely! We welcome visitors, especially aspiring farmers. Please call or WhatsApp us in advance to schedule a visit. We can show you our coops, feeding routines, and answer your questions.

Do you offer payment plans?

For large orders (100+ chicks), we can discuss payment plans. We accept cash, mobile money (MTN, Airtel), and bank transfers.
