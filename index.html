FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you don't find what you need, WhatsApp us directly.

Where is Unified Farm BLM located? +

We are located in Mpigi Town, Uganda, along the Masaka Road — approximately 26 km from Kampala. You can visit our farm or we can deliver to your location.

Do you deliver outside Kampala? +

Yes! We deliver to Kampala, Entebbe, Jinja, Mukono, Masaka, Mbarara, and all major towns across Uganda. Delivery fees depend on distance and order size.

What is your day-old chick survival rate? +

Our day-old chicks have a 95%+ survival rate when proper care is followed. We provide a free care guide with every purchase and offer follow-up support via WhatsApp.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept Mobile Money (MTN & Airtel) and bank transfers. Contact us for payment details when you place your order.
