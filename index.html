❓ Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before placing your order. Can't find your answer? Chat with us below!

🐣 Do you deliver chicks? +

Yes, we deliver day-old chicks and one-month brooded chicks across Uganda. Our farm is located in Mpigi on Masaka Road, 26 km from Kampala, making delivery to central Uganda fast and affordable. We use specialized transport to ensure chicks arrive healthy. Contact us for a delivery quote to your area!

📦 How do I place an order? +

You can place an order via WhatsApp, phone call, or email. We recommend calling +256 708 813 419 for fastest response. For large orders, we require a 50% deposit to confirm your booking. Walk-ins are also welcome at our farm in Mpigi!

💳 What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept Mobile Money (MTN & Airtel), bank transfers, and cash on delivery for local orders within Kampala and surrounding areas. For bulk orders, we also accept installment payments. All payments are secure and confirmed before delivery.

🌾 Do you offer farm advice? +

Absolutely! We provide free consultation on poultry management, feeding programs, and disease prevention for all our customers. Follow our Farming Guides section for detailed articles, or chat with us directly using the orange button at the bottom right of your screen.
