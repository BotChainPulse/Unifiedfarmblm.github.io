❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you don't see yours, message us on WhatsApp!

Where is your farm located? +

We are located in Mpigi District, along the Kampala-Masaka highway. We also deliver to Kampala, Entebbe, and surrounding areas.

Do you deliver? +

Yes! We deliver across Uganda within 24-48 hours. Delivery fees depend on your location. Orders above UGX 200,000 get free delivery within Kampala.

Are your chicks vaccinated? +

Absolutely. All our chicks come fully vaccinated against Newcastle disease, Gumboro, and Marek's disease. We provide a vaccination certificate with every purchase.

What breeds do you sell? +

We specialize in improved Kuroiler, layers (Hy-Line Brown), and broilers (Cobb 500). All breeds are selected for high productivity in Uganda's climate.

Can I visit the farm? +

Yes! We welcome farm visits by appointment. It's a great way to see our operations firsthand and learn from our team. Contact us to schedule.

How do I place an order? +

Click the "Order Now" button, send us a WhatsApp message, or call us directly. We'll confirm your order and arrange delivery.
