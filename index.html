Raising Healthy Chickens, Empowering Farmers

Unified Farm BLM is a family-run poultry farm located in Mpigi, Uganda. We specialize in producing fresh organic eggs, breeding healthy day-old chicks, and providing expert guidance to both new and experienced poultry farmers.

Our mission is to deliver quality poultry products while promoting sustainable farming practices in our community. Every bird is raised with care, every egg is collected fresh, and every customer becomes part of our farming family.
