What We Sell

Everything below is produced on our farm or sourced from our own breeding stock. Visit us in Mpigi, or reach out through the contact page to place an order — we advise calling ahead for day-old chicks so we can plan your hatch date.

Best Seller

Fresh Eggs

Organic, farm-fresh eggs collected daily from our free-roaming layers. Available by the tray or in bulk for shops, schools and restaurants. Freshness guaranteed — our eggs never sit in storage.

Day-Old Chicks

Healthy, vaccinated day-old chicks from our own breeding stock. Strong starts make strong flocks — we hatch to order so your chicks arrive fresh and vigorous. Book your hatch date in advance.

Less Risk

Brooded Chicks

Past the fragile brooding stage and ready for your coop. Ideal if you want to skip the delicate first weeks — these birds are hardened, feeding well and vaccinated on schedule.

Layer Birds

Point-of-lay pullets for farmers who want eggs sooner, not months from now. Raised on quality feed with full vaccination records available.

For Gardens

Chicken Manure

Rich organic fertilizer for gardens and crop fields. A natural boost for matooke, vegetables and coffee — available by the sack from the farm.
